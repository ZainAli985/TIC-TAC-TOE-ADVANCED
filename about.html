Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple yet engaging two-player game where players take turns marking a square in a 3x3 grid. The objective is to get three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

This classic game is perfect for all ages and helps develop strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. Enjoy the thrill of competition with friends or family!

Rules

  1. Players take turns placing their marks (X or O) in empty squares.
  2. The first player to get three marks in a row wins.
  3. If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
  4. Players can choose to play multiple rounds.
  5. Have fun!
